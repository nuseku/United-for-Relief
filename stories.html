Countless lives have been lost, and countless more have been forever changed by the tragedy that has befallen us. As we try to make sense of the devastation and find ways to help those affected, it can be difficult to fully grasp the scope of the suffering. But through these images, we catch a glimpse of the heartbreak, the loss, and the unimaginable challenges faced by so many. They remind us that this is not just a news story, not just a distant tragedy, but a human crisis of unimaginable scale and significance. May they inspire us to take action, to show compassion, and to do all we can to bring hope and healing to those who are hurting.
